A little bit about me.

Orginally from Evansville, Indiana and currently a resident of New York City. I graduated from The University of North Carolina Charlotte with a Bachelors Degree. After graduation I moved to Los Angeles where i pursued my career as an actress. Two years ago I relocated to the East Coast. I am currently studying Front-end Web Design at General Assembly and plan to pursue this new career full time.

I began coding in my spare time, and I was hooked after building my first website. I began studying front-end web design as I enjoyed writing code and seeing the final product of my HTML, CSS & JavaScript. I also believe that my years in customer service gives me an edge on understanding what people want and being able to articulate that so I can build exactly what my customers desires. I am interested in front-end web design as a career, and I am thrilled about continuing learning and refining my skills.

Hand-Coded HTML

Writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 is my specialty so as to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on the current best practices.

Designs into Code

You can trust me to take a designer's PSD and wireframes and quickly & accurately convert them into webpages that are pixel-perfect match.
